Privacy Policy – DocDoc App

Effective date: [20 Feb 2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how DocDoc (the “App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services. By using DocDoc, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

1. What DocDoc Does

DocDoc is a mental-wellbeing companion app that helps you:

  • Track your mood over time
  • Recognize emotions using your device camera and on-device AI models
  • Receive personalized recommendations and exercises
  • Access AI one-on-one sessions
  • View mood analytics and trends
  • Optionally subscribe to premium features via in-app purchases

DocDoc is not a medical device and does not provide medical or psychiatric diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified professional for medical or mental-health advice.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the App.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account information

  • Name, email address, password or authentication tokens (depending on your sign-up method)
  • Stored in our backend and/or secure storage to allow login and account management

Profile & personal information

  • Optional profile details such as date of birth and other personal preferences
  • Used to personalize your experience (e.g., greetings, analytics segments)

Mood entries & notes

  • Mood selections (emojis, labels such as “happy”, “sad”, etc.)
  • Optional notes/comments you add about how you feel
  • Used to build your mood history, analytics, and recommendations

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device and app usage data

  • Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Basic usage logs (which screens are visited, feature usage)
  • Used to improve stability, performance, and understand how features are used

Camera data (for emotion recognition)

  • The App uses your camera to analyze facial expressions in real time through on-device machine-learning models.
  • Camera frames are processed on your device for emotion detection.
  • We do not store your raw camera images on our servers.
  • We may store the recognized emotion label (e.g., “happy”, “stressed”) and a timestamp as part of your mood history.

2.3 Payment & Subscription Information

In-app purchases and subscriptions

  • We use RevenueCat and the Apple App Store billing system to manage subscriptions and purchases.
  • We may receive:
    • A non-sensitive identifier for your subscription
    • Product IDs, purchase status (active, canceled, expired), and subscription periods
    • Region/country associated with your App Store account (from Apple)
  • We do not store or have access to your full payment card details. Payments are processed by Apple.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
    • Authenticate you and keep you logged in
    • Save and display your mood history, AI sessions context, and recommendations
    • Process subscriptions and restore purchases
  • Personalize your experience
    • Tailor exercises, recommendations, and AI sessions to your mood and history
    • Show relevant insights and analytics
  • Improve and secure the App
    • Analyze feature usage and performance
    • Fix bugs and improve user experience
    • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Communicate with you
    • Send important service and account notifications
    • Respond to your support requests

We only use your information for the purposes described in this Policy, and where we have a legal basis to do so (such as your consent, contract performance, or legitimate interests).
